What is Lost/Thread?

Lost/Thread is an immersive, cinematic audio drama blending mystery, psychological horror, and existential themes. Inspired by enigmatic disappearances and eerie urban legends, the story unfolds through unsettling recordings, cryptic messages, and reality-warping encounters.
