About the Project

Lost/Thread is a mystery-horror audio drama about a group of college friends chasing the unexplained—until reality starts to unravel around them. Created, written, and directed by Tristen, with sound design by Advait Zambre, this project blends psychological horror, supernatural elements, and existential themes into a haunting, immersive experience.
